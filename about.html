Our Story

Graphix Iowa was founded with the mission to help local businesses thrive by providing high-quality and affordable graphic design services. With a passion for creativity and a dedication to our community, we bring innovative design solutions to life.

Since our founding, we've had the privilege of working with numerous local businesses, helping them create compelling brands and engaging online presences. Our journey began in a small home office and has grown to become a trusted name in the local design community.

Our Mission, Values, and Culture

At Graphix Iowa, our mission is to empower small businesses through exceptional design. We operate on a set of core values that define our company culture and guide our work:

  • Integrity: We believe in honest and transparent business practices.
  • Customer Focus: Our clients are at the heart of everything we do. We strive to exceed their expectations in every project.
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and innovation to deliver unique and effective design solutions.
  • Collaboration: We work closely with our clients, fostering a collaborative environment to bring their visions to life.
  • Quality: We are committed to excellence in every aspect of our work, ensuring high-quality outcomes that make a lasting impact.
